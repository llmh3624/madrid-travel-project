Why Palma?

Palma: Beaches, History, and Vibrant Charm

Palma, the captivating capital city of the Balearic Islands, is a perfect travel destination offering stunning beaches, rich history, a charming old town, and a vibrant atmosphere. Visitors can bask in the beauty of picturesque coastal stretches like Playa de Palma, explore the Gothic magnificence of Palma Cathedral, and wander through the enchanting alleys of the old town. The city's cosmopolitan vibe is evident in its diverse culinary scene, vibrant nightlife, and excellent shopping options.

Furthermore, Palma serves as a gateway to other breathtaking destinations on the island of Mallorca, allowing travelers to explore charming coastal towns and captivating natural landscapes. With its irresistible combination of natural beauty, historical charm, and lively ambiance, Palma promises an unforgettable travel experience.

My favorite restaurants in Palma

Calitxo

Experience the heart of Palma at a renowned restaurant praised for its exceptional paellas and delicious entrees, offering a memorable dining experience in a prime city center location. Don't forget to try sangria!

Address:

Carrer del Banc de l'Oli, 14

What I like about it

This is one of the best paella spots in Palma that is affordable and delicious. You can't go wrong!

Temple Natura

Uncover the charm of an amazing restaurant hidden within an old house's secret garden, where every dish is a delectable and nutritious vegetarian option, providing a delightful and healthy dining experience.

Address:

Carrer del Temple, 4

What I like about it

Not always easy to get a seat but the food is always tasty and the staff are so friendly.

Dragon Sushi

Experience an extraordinary sushi buffet that surpasses expectations with its delectable rolls, tantalizing Asian entrees, and a wide variety of other Asian dishes, creating an unforgettable and satisfying dining adventure.

Address:

Carrer de Gilabert de Centelles, 4

What I like about it

It is one of the most affordable sushi restaurant and if you go on a Wednesday the first drink is free.
